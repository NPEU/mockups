Out-of-hours

In the case of urgent out-of-hours queries please phone 0800 138 5451. When you call this Freephone number you will go through to a call centre which provides 24 hour emergency call service. They will ask for your name, the hospital you are calling from, your full phone number, and the name of the trial (NeoCLEAR), before they are able to address your urgent query.

N.B. The out-of-hours service will not be able to perform remote randomisation in the case of internet failure at centres.

If your query is not urgent please email neoclear@npeu.ox.ac.uk or leave a message on the study answerphone 01865 289278.
