PPPI

Parent, Patient and Public Involvement (PPPI) is at the heart of our vision for the PRU-MNHC and is embedded in every aspect of strategy and delivery. Co-investigators Rachel Plachcinski and Charlotte Bevan are the co-chairs of our PPPI stakeholder network meetings, and support wider stakeholder engagement for our overall strategy and individual projects. Through our PPPI strategy we aim to identify effective ways to empower service users to shape the work of the PRU-MNHC through shared decision making. The integration of the perceptions and experiences of patients, the public, parents and families in our work is essential to produce high quality, inclusive research, policy and care.
